Sean Van Echelpoel

I am Sean Van Echelpoel, a visual arts student who currently
lives in a small town nearby Antwerp, Belgium.

Over the past years, I am working towards a synergy between
different mediums such as photography, videography and
graphic design, With a love for depiction through installation.

In addition to my studio, I am frequently involved in modeling,
whereby the influence of fashion often translates into my work.
